- **Why they belong:** Becton is this entry's subject and one of the most consequential Black military-integration figures of the post-WWII era. - **Transcript evidence:** Pervasive across entire 129KB transcript; hundreds of direct references.

- **Name:** Charles Bernard Rangel (1930–2023)
- **Role:** U.S. Army veteran (9th Infantry Regiment, Korea; Bronze Star at Battle of Kunu-ri, November 1950); later 23-term U.S. Representative from Harlem, New York (1971–2017); long-serving Chairman of the House Ways and Means Committee
- **Why they belong:** Rangel served in the same regiment (9th Infantry) and the same Korean War campaign (Battle of Kunu-ri, November 1950) as Becton.
