**Subject**: Charles McLaurin (b. 25 December 1939, Jackson, MS) — Lanier High School, US Army Reserve (Fort Jackson, SC, 1955); recruited by James Bevel and Medgar Evers in 1961–62; in August 1962 became one of the first SNCC Mississippi Delta organizers under Bob Moses, going from Jackson to Ruleville (Sunflower County) with Landy McNair and Charlie Cobb. **The person who recruited Fannie Lou Hamer into the SNCC voter-registration movement** (Aug 1962, in Ruleville; he asked her to come to Indianola the next day to register; she said yes). The longest transcript in the corpus to date.
