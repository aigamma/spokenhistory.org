**Interviewer / Date**: Joe Mosnier (Southern Oral History Program at UNC Chapel Hill) — Tuesday 31 May 2011, Selma, AL
**Cameraman**: John Bishop
**Subject**: Annie Pearl Avery — born Birmingham, AL ~1944. Lived with her great-aunt in Pittsburgh during her early childhood (Emmett Till era). SNCC field organizer from 1961; arrested in Marietta GA, Birmingham, Albany GA, Danville VA (90 days in solitary for contempt), Mississippi (Clarksdale), Natchez. Project director in Hale County, AL on voter registration. Arrested on Bloody Sunday — the only marcher arrested that day (her marker that the police's intent was brutalization, not arrest). Marched on March 7, 9, and the full Selma-to-Montgomery final march.
