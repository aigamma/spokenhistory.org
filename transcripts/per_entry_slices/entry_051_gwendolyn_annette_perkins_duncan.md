**Subject**: Gwendolyn Annette Perkins Duncan — fifth-generation Lincolnville (St. Augustine FL Black neighborhood) native, born June 28, 1956; daughter of Mrs. Perkins (mother of eight girls, no boys); great-great-grand-daughter of Thomas A.
