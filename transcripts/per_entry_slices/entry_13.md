### 13. Carrie Lamar Young (PARTIAL)

**Source**: `transcripts/raw/Carrie Lamar Young_interview_20250704_181721/`
**Interviewer / Date**: Joe Mosnier (Southern Oral History Program / UNC) — Monday 26 September 2011, Little Rock, AR (her home). Cameraman John Bishop.
**Subject**: Carrie Lamar Young — born early 1940s, Barton, AR; raised in Lexa, AR (Phillips County), then West Helena, AR; SNCC field worker in West Helena starting ~1965 with the SNCC Arkansas Project under Bill Hansen, Howard Himmelbaum, Jerry Casey, et al. Married Howard Himmelbaum (white SNCC organizer) in 1967. Lawsuit pioneer — won two major class-action discrimination cases in Pulaski County, AR (against AMF and National Old Line Life Insurance). First Black office worker in the Arkansas State Capitol administrative office (under Republican Governor Winthrop Rockefeller). Later a Southwestern Bell engineer for 14 years.
