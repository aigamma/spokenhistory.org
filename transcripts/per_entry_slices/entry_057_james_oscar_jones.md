- **Black-farm-owner intergenerational-defense canonical first-person**: Jones's account of his parents (Ernest and Vic) and aunt and uncle showing up at the cotton gin with "double-barred shotguns and two shells between their fingers" to defend the family's accurate cotton-weight is canonical first-person material on the rare armed-defense tactic in agricultural-economy racial-discrimination contexts.
