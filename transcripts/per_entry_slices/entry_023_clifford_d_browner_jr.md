**Interviewer / Date**: Dr. Hasan Kwame Jeffries (Ohio State University) and Olya Burroughs Henlein (?) — Saturday 9 March 2013, Albany State University, Albany, GA.
**Subject**: Clifford D. Browner Jr. (b. 5 July 1946) — Sasser, Terrell County, GA. Member of Mt. Olive Baptist Church, which became the Sasser mass-meeting site for the SW Georgia Movement after Z.T. Mathews and Klansmen invaded the prior meeting at Mt. Olive in summer 1962 — the church was **burned to the ground** that night by white nightriders. Wrote a famous letter (with classmate Clovis Jones) as a high schooler to the white school superintendent demanding new textbooks; nearly expelled. Drafted to Vietnam-era army (Germany, 1964–66); GM auto-assembly worker in Flint and then Doraville, GA for 20 years before retirement.
