**Subject**: Cecilia Suyat Marshall (1928–2022) — widow of US Supreme Court Justice Thurgood Marshall. Born and raised in Hawaii; moved to New York City in the late 1940s; placed by the employment office at the NAACP National Office; worked as secretary to Gloucester Current (Director of Branches) from ~1948 onward; married Thurgood Marshall in 1955 after his first wife's death. First-hand witness to the NAACP National Office's strategic work on *Brown v. Board* and the Marshall family's residential integration of Morningside Gardens (NYC) and later Lake Barcroft (Virginia).
