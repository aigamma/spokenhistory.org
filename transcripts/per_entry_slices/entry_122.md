**Subject**: Virginia Simms George (b. ~1940, Newport News VA), canonical Virginia Union University class of 1963 + canonical Feb 1960 Richmond VA Thalhimers Department Store sit-in arrest under Charles Sherrod + Frank Pinkston student-leadership; lifelong HR-corporate / Alfred University Women's Leadership Center / LINKS Springfield MA mentor + Massachusetts community organizer. Educated at Virginia Union University (canonical Black HBCU Richmond VA) 1959-1963, BA in History (intended for law school).
